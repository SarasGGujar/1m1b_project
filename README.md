üõë Problem Statement

The Illegal Dumping Problem in local neighborhood or nearby places causes significant environmental and health crisis. In metropolitan cities, Garbage fires, toxic pollutants like PM 2.5, Dioxins in air, are causing many issues to People. These leads worsen effect to residents living nearby. Due to lack of real time data or Traditional approach to handle the waste type causes the rise in waste management and health issues. This Issue could be solved by AI by bridging the gap between people and municipal authorities by instant identifying the waste and reporting it. This will ensure the Health and well-being and making Sustainable Cities and Communities.


üí° Solution

This Solution helps the problem of toxic, not organized, recyclable trash or other waste type to report to municipal authorities. The user will Click and photo of dumping site which the AI will classify the type of waste and it will automaticall draft the report to authorities, including location and urgency level. The AI will be powered by IBM Granite 3.2.
